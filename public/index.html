Organic Sanitary Pad and Natural Essence Diapers Bank

The girl child and women's health and hygiene has been one of the key concerns in Nigeria. The awareness on menstrual hygiene and usage of Sanitary pad is virtually absent especially in the  rural areas.  Poor menstrual hygiene can cause fungal infections, repeated infections, cervical cancer and they become vulnerable to infertility. Unfortunately reproductive hygiene education and sexual education is not part of educational curriculum in Nigeria.
